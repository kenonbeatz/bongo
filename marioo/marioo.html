Marioo

About

Marioo (born Kibiti, Pwani, Tanzania) is a Bongo Flava singer, also a Tanzanian composer and songwriter. Marioo has composed songs and sold them as a song sung by a Tanzanian musician named Nandy ¨The African princess¨ called ¨Don't deceive youang She is also the author of Jux's song "Unaniweza". His first song is Dar Kugumu, recorded in 2017, and became well known as the number one song in almost all radio and television stations in Tanzania. The Marioo tribe is Mndengereko. He has also successfully collaborated with artists from various countries including South Africa.
